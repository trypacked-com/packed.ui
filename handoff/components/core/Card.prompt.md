The warm surface container — Packed's workhorse layout primitive. White surface, soft border, generous radius.

```jsx
<Card interactive elevation="sm">
  <h3>Lisbon escape</h3>
  <p>5 days · 2 travellers</p>
</Card>
```

`elevation`: `flat | xs | sm | md | lg`. Set `interactive` to lift on hover, `padded={false}` for full-bleed media cards.
